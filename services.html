FAQ

Common Questions

During your free consultation, we'll evaluate your specific needs, bone structure, and lifestyle to recommend the best option. As a general guide: Essential and Comfort tiers work great for straightforward replacements, while Premium and Elite are best for patients who want the most natural appearance and longest-lasting results.

Acrylic partials can often be ready the same day. Complete dentures and cast-metal partials typically take 2–3 weeks from the impression appointment. We'll give you a specific timeline at your consultation.

Yes — we work with most major dental insurance plans. We also offer financing through Sunbit for patients who need flexible payment options. Call us or ask at your consultation and we'll verify your coverage.

All packages include a warranty against teeth popping out and breakage under normal use. The warranty period ranges from 6 months (Essential) to 5 years (Elite). It does not cover accidental damage such as dropping the denture.

Many minor repairs — such as re-attaching a tooth or fixing a hairline crack — can be completed the same day. Call ahead to describe the issue and we'll let you know if same-day service is possible.
